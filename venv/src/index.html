Who are we?

We at STUNet aim to provide a solution for the problem which currently facing by the colleges and organizers to find the guest lecturers.

Why we want to do this?

Currently there is a gap with finding the speakers/professors/lecturers. There are a lots of excellent professors, Teachers, Speakers who loves to share the knowledge with people, but the problem is colleges were unable to identify them. There no platform for speakers where they have more exposure to organisers. Due to this even if they are interest to speak, they weren't able to as there is no exposure.

What we want to do?

We wanted to created a one stop platform where colleges, directors, event organisers to find the guest lecturers who are willing to share their knowledge.

What is our Mission?

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the speakers, organizers or colleges and students by creating a friendly community to share the knowledge.

What is it and How it works?

The STUNet is a platform for speakers who are passionate to share the knowledge via guest lectures. As a speaker, you will register in the platform. College principals or directors or event organisers who are already registered in the platform will invite the speakers for guest lectures to their colleges or events or communities.

We benefit the speakers who loves to share the knowledge and they can earn money if they were invited for any guest lectures

What does this cost?

This is an absolutely free service for everyone. You just need to register and make use of it.
